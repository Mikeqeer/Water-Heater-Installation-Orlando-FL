Common Questions About Water Heater Service in Orlando

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things homeowners ask us.

How much does water heater installation cost in Orlando? ▾
Water heater installation in Orlando typically ranges from $800 to $1,800 for a standard tank unit, depending on the size, brand, and complexity of the installation. Tankless water heater installation in Orlando can range from $1,200 to $3,000 or more. We offer transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a free estimate tailored to your home.
How long does water heater installation take? ▾
A standard water heater replacement in Orlando typically takes 2–4 hours from start to finish. Tankless water heater installations may take 4–6 hours due to the additional venting and gas/electrical work involved. Our technicians are efficient and will minimize disruption to your home.
Do you offer same-day water heater service in Orlando? ▾
Yes! Same-day water heater service in Orlando is one of our specialties. In most cases, we can have a certified technician at your home within 1–2 hours of your call. We also offer 24/7 emergency water heater service for urgent situations like leaks or complete failures.
What brands of water heaters do you install? ▾
We install and service all major water heater brands, including Rheem, A.O. Smith, Bradford White, Navien, Rinnai, Noritz, State, and many more. If you have a specific brand in mind, just let us know — we'll source it for you at a competitive price.
Is a permit required for water heater installation in Orlando? ▾
In many cases, yes — a permit is required for water heater installation in Orlando under Florida building codes. Don't worry: we handle all permitting on your behalf. This ensures your installation is code-compliant, safe, and protects your homeowner's warranty.
